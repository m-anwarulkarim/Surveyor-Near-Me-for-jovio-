RICS LEVEL 2 HOME SURVEY

RICS Level 2 Home Survey

The RICS Level 2 Home Survey is designed for conventional properties in reasonable condition. It provides a clear overview of the property's condition using an easy-to-understand traffic light rating system.

What the Survey Covers

External Elements

  • • Roof coverings (tiles, slates, chimneys)
  • • Gutters, downpipes, and rainwater goods
  • • External walls (brickwork, render, cladding)
  • • Windows and doors
  • • Fascia boards and soffits
  • • External joinery and finishes
  • • Boundaries (walls, fences, gates)

Internal & Services

  • • Ceilings and internal walls
  • • Floors and floor structure (visible areas)
  • • Windows and internal joinery
  • • Fireplaces and chimney breasts
  • • Signs of damp, cracking, or movement
  • • Electrical installations (visual only)
  • • Gas supply, heating systems, and drainage where visible

What You Receive

  • • Condition ratings (1–3 traffic light system)
  • • Identification of urgent defects
  • • Risks that may affect value
  • • Advice on repairs and maintenance
  • • Optional market valuation and rebuild cost

Who It’s For

  • • Buyers of modern or standard construction homes
  • • Properties in generally good condition
RICS LEVEL 3 HOME SURVEY

RICS Level 3 Home Survey

The RICS Level 3 Home Survey is a detailed and comprehensive inspection suitable for older, altered, or complex properties.

What the Survey Covers

External Elements

  • • Roof structure and coverings (including loft inspection)
  • • Chimneys and parapets
  • • Walls (structure, movement, cracking)
  • • Rainwater systems and drainage
  • • External joinery, windows, and doors
  • • Boundaries and outbuildings

Internal & Services

  • • Floors and subfloor (where accessible)
  • • Walls, ceilings, and structural elements
  • • Staircases and load-bearing elements
  • • Detailed inspection of defects
  • • Heating, Electrical & Plumbing systems overview

What You Receive

  • • Detailed description of construction and condition
  • • Diagnosis of defects and causes
  • • Repair options and recommendations
  • • Maintenance advice and future risks
  • • Optional valuation

Who It’s For

  • • Older or period properties
  • • Buildings with extensions or loft conversions
  • • Properties in poor condition
Drone Roof Survey

Safe, Efficient Roof Inspections Without Scaffolding

At IQSURV, we offer advanced drone roof surveys to safely inspect hard-to-reach areas without the need for scaffolding or ladders. Using high-resolution aerial imaging, we provide a detailed assessment of your roof condition quickly, efficiently, and cost-effectively.

Benefits of a Drone Survey

  • • No need for scaffolding or expensive access equipment
  • • Safe inspection of difficult or hazardous areas
  • • Fast turnaround time
  • • Cost-effective compared to traditional access methods
  • • Minimal disruption to occupants

What We Inspect

Roof Coverings

  • • Missing/damaged tiles/slates
  • • General wear & deterioration
  • • Flat roof coverings & membranes

Roof Structure

  • • Ridge tiles and hips
  • • Chimneys, flashings, & parapets
  • • Roof junctions and detailing

Rainwater Goods

  • • Gutters and downpipes
  • • Blockages, leaks, or misalignment

Other Features

  • • Fascias and soffits
  • • Solar panels (if present)
  • • High-level masonry & cladding

What You Receive

  • • High-res images & aerial footage
  • • Clear condition assessment
  • • Identification of defects & risks
  • • Professional repair recommendations

When Do You Need It?

  • • After extreme weather/storms
  • • Before buying or selling
  • • As part of planned maintenance
  • • When access is restricted
VALUATION SURVEYS

Valuation Surveys (RICS Red Book Compliant)

All of our valuation reports are prepared in accordance with RICS Red Book standards, ensuring they are compliant, reliable, and accepted by lenders, HMRC, courts, and other authorities.

Who Needs Reports?

  • • Homeowners buying/selling
  • • Landlords and investors
  • • Solicitors & Legal professionals
  • • Tax advisors and accountants
  • • Housing associations
  • • Probate or divorce matters

Our Valuation Services Include

Market Valuation

Assessment of current market value for buying, selling, or refinancing.

Probate Valuation

Required for inheritance tax purposes when administering an estate.

Capital Gains Tax

Used to calculate tax liabilities when disposing of assets.

Help to Buy Valuation

Required when repaying an equity loan or selling the property.

Shared Ownership Valuation

Needed for staircasing or selling shared ownership properties and must meet strict housing association requirements.

What You Receive

RICS Red Book compliant report
Market evidence & analysis
Clear valuation rationale
Suitable for official & legal use
